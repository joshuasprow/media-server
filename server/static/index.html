Jackett
The indexer. This service checks torrent sites for the latest releases. It stores links to those releases so that the PVR services know what to download.

Plex
The media player. Use it to watch videos on your phone, a web browser, or Chromecast.

Radarr
The movie PVR. Tag movies for download and Radarr will handle downloading them and letting Plex know that they're available.

Sonarr
The TV PVR. Tag series for download and Sonarr will keep track of upcoming episodes. You can also manually download past episodes and seasons.

Deluge
The download client. This service handles the business of downloading torrents.
